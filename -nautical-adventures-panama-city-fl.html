Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best time of year to experience Shell Island's natural beauty?

The best time of year to experience Shell Island's natural beauty is typically during the warmer months, from spring to fall, when wildlife activity is at its peak.

Are there restroom facilities available on Shell Island?

Restroom facilities are available on some Shell Island excursions or at designated spots on the island.

Can I collect seashells during the Shell Island tours?

Yes, collecting seashells is allowed during the Shell Island tours, but it's essential to follow guidelines to preserve the natural environment.

Can I bring my own food and beverages on the Shell Island Boat Charters?

Policies regarding bringing food and beverages may differ, so it's advisable to check with the specific Shell Island Boat Charters operator.

Can I participate in Shell Island Excursions if I don't know how to swim?

Non-swimmers can still participate in many Shell Island Excursions, as safety measures are in place, and life jackets are provided for added security.
